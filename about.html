A little about myself

When I'm not coding I have a couple of hobbies I like to do with friends or by myself

Rock Climbing

I like to go indoor rock climbing or bouldering in other terms. I like to stay active since most of my time I spend around sitting on a computer either learning code or just watching anime or shows. I don't go as often as I like to due to work scheduling but its a good where to have fun and still be able to get a work out in. Plus its fun when you have friends join in too, as it not only helps motivates one another to push past our limits but time flies when you're tring to beat your firends in a little climbing competition.

Korean BBQ

Korean BBQ/ KBBQ is always my go to when someone ask where I want to eat. Though some people may not like the aspect of cooking your own food that you pay for. I for myself enjoy that aspect, since for one I love cooking in general so I'm always the one in my friend group cooking whenever we all go out. Another reason why I like KBBQ so much is because the social setting. Its like having a home cooked meal with all your friends without actually being at home. Also its all you can eat so if I'm gonna pay about $30 to $40 for food might as well get all you can eat.
